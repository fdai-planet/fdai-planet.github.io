Caltech WAVE

The The Caltech WAVE Program supports undergraduate research opportunities for students from underrepresented backgrounds, including racially minoritized communities, women, first-generation college students, geographically underrepresented students, educationally or financially disadvantaged students, and students with disabilities. Through this program, I had the privilege of working with Sarah Lange, a bright and driven sophomore. Together, we compiled exoplanet radial velocity datasets from the literature and developed a publicly available Python package, EZRV, to facilitate access and enable population studies. Over the course of our 12-week project, Sarah progressed from writing her very first lines of code to publishing her first Python package on GitHub.
